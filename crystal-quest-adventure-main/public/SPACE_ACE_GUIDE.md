## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY

### Controls

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| **Arrow Keys / WASD** | Move your Ace-1 Interceptor in 8 directions |
| **SPACE** | Fire your current weapon |
| **Q** | Switch between weapon systems |
| **X** | Unleash the **Nova Charge** (when fully charged) |
| **ENTER** | Start game / Continue / Retry |

---

## ⚔️ WEAPON SYSTEMS

### 🔵 Pulse Laser (Default)
Your standard-issue energy weapon. Reliable, focused, and upgradeable. Fires a single concentrated beam straight ahead. Great for picking off individual targets.

### 🟢 Twin-Spread
Switches your output to a triple-spread pattern. Covers more vertical space but each projectile deals slightly less damage. Perfect for dealing with formations.

### 💜 Nova Charge (Special)
The Ace-1's experimental Hyper-Core weapon. Collect **Star Fragments** ⭐ dropped by destroyed enemies to fill your Nova Charge meter. When full, press **X** to unleash a devastating screen-clearing blast that destroys all regular enemies and deals massive damage to bosses. Use it wisely!

---

## 👾 ENEMY DATABASE

### 🟢 Zog Scouts
Small green UFOs that attack in wave formations. They move in sinusoidal patterns and fire single shots. Individually weak, but dangerous in numbers.
- **HP:** Low
- **Points:** 100
- **Threat Level:** ⚠️

### 🔴 Pulse Mines
Stationary-drifting red hazards that detonate into 8-directional shrapnel when you get too close. Shoot them from a distance or dodge their blast radius.
- **HP:** Medium
- **Points:** 150
- **Threat Level:** ⚠️⚠️

### 💀 Level Boss: The Ring-Stalker
A massive armored cruiser that appears when the level timer runs out. Fires spreads of energy bolts and has heavy armor plating. Each level's boss is tougher than the last.
- **HP:** Increases per level (30 → 150)
- **Points:** 500 × Level Number + 1000 completion bonus
- **Threat Level:** ☠️☠️☠️

## 🌌 THE FIVE SECTORS

Each level is **timed**. Survive the onslaught, then face the boss!

### Level 1 — SECTOR 7 OUTSKIRTS
*Where it all begins.* Scout formations test your reflexes. Get comfortable with the controls.
- **Duration:** 45 seconds
- **Enemies:** Zog Scouts
- **Boss HP:** 30

### Level 2 — ASTEROID BELT
*The minefield.* Pulse Mines join the fray. Watch your spacing!
- **Duration:** 50 seconds
- **Enemies:** Scouts + Mines
- **Boss HP:** 50

### Level 3 — RINGS OF SATURN
*No place to hide.* Faster enemies, tighter formations. The Ring-Stalker uses Saturn's rings as cover.
- **Duration:** 55 seconds
- **Enemies:** Scouts + Mines (faster)
- **Boss HP:** 75

### Level 4 — NEBULA CORE
*Visibility drops, danger rises.* Enemies are tougher and more aggressive. Your piloting skills will be tested.
- **Duration:** 60 seconds
- **Enemies:** Scouts + Mines (much faster)
- **Boss HP:** 100

### Level 5 — ZOG MOTHERSHIP
*The final assault.* Everything the Zog Empire has, thrown at you at once. Relentless waves. An ultra-armored boss. This is it.
- **Duration:** 70 seconds
- **Enemies:** All types (maximum speed & HP)
- **Boss HP:** 150

## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Stay mobile.** Never hover in one spot — the bullet patterns will box you in.
2. **Use Spread for formations.** When scouts come in V-patterns, switch to Twin-Spread.
3. **Save your Nova.** Don't waste it on scouts. Keep it charged for the boss fight.
4. **Learn mine distances.** They detonate at ~60px. Shoot them from afar.
5. **Boss dodge pattern:** Move vertically in circles. Their spread shots leave gaps.
6. **Collect every Star Fragment.** Each one fills 25% of your Nova meter.
7. **After getting hit, you're invincible briefly.** Use that time to reposition, not attack.
